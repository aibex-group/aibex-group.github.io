# AI Generators for Handwritten SVG Output

**Contact:** <a href="mailto:marco.peer@unifr.ch">Marco Peer</a>

Note: The thesis is in collaboration with Forensisches Institut Zürich.

## Overview
AI-based handwriting generators have made significant progress in recent years (Figure 1). However, most existing systems produce raster graphics. For reproducing handwriting with a pen plotter on paper, stroke paths rather than pixel-based images are required. Such information can be represented in SVG data (single-line vector graphics).
This master’s thesis investigates whether AI-based handwriting generators exist that can produce such vector-based outputs. Furthermore, the thesis evaluates how convincing the generated handwriting appears when physically reproduced on paper.

## Objectives
- Review the state of the art in AI-based handwriting generation  
- Identify AI generators capable of producing single-line handwriting as SVG output  
- Investigate methods to convert raster-based AI outputs into SVG stroke paths if direct solutions are not available  
- Generate handwriting samples in the style of approximately 20 individuals  
- Reproduce generated handwriting using a pen plotting device  
- Evaluate the perceived authenticity of the generated and plotted handwriting in a study with laypersons  

## Methodology
- **Literature Review:** Survey existing approaches to AI-based handwriting generation  
- **Technology Analysis:** Identify tools capable of producing SVG-based handwriting  
- **SVG Conversion:** Develop and test workflows to convert AI-generated images into plausible stroke paths  
- **Handwriting Generation:** Generate handwriting samples for approximately 20 individuals  
- **Physical Reproduction:** Plot selected samples using a pen plotting device  
- **Evaluation Study:** Assess whether laypersons can distinguish between plotted AI-generated samples and genuine handwritten samples  
- **Reporting:** Document results in the master’s thesis and final presentation
